About Me

Bio

I'm Ahmed M.M. Elsayed, a Particle Physicist. I am passionate about particle physics.

Education

  • 2019–2023: Bachelor of Science in Applied Physics, Sichuan University of Science and Engineering. Thesis: “Neutrino Mass Measurements.”
  • 2023–Present: Master of Science in Nuclear and Particle Physics, University of Science and Technology of China. Research focus on neutrino physics with extensive work on the Type III Seesaw Model at the LHC (CERN, Switzerland). Future plans include investigating Type I and II Seesaw Models and developing a background identification and selection algorithm for Beyond Standard Model searches at the LHC.
